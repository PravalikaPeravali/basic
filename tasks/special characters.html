Special Characters and Symbols in HTML

  • © - Copyright Symbol: Represents copyright ownership.
  • < - Less Than Symbol: Used for indicating less than in mathematical or logical operations.
  • > - Greater Than Symbol: Used for indicating greater than in mathematical or logical operations.
  • & - Ampersand Symbol: Used to represent the ampersand character itself in HTML.
  • € - Euro Symbol: Represents the Euro currency symbol.
  • ™ - Trademark Symbol: Indicates a trademarked word, phrase, or symbol.
